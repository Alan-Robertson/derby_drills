\subsection{Four Wall Pack}
\label{drill:four_wall/pack}

This drill requires six skaters - four blockers, a jammer and an offence.

Three blockers start in a three wall, the fourth blocker begins holding the offence 10 feet behind.
The jammer begins in the wall. 


The goal of the fourth blocker is to keep the wall in play, the offence may attempt to move up, or may attempt to stop.
Depending on the familiarity of the blockers with the drill, the jammer may facilitate or actively jam.

\begin{itemize}
\item If the offence attempts to move up, the ``pack manager" should continue blocking them, while directing their own pack to move up the track. Their goal is to keep their pack at least 10 feet ahead of the offence.
\item If the offence attempts to stop to draw pack out, then the pack manager can instead attempt to drive the offence up, maintaining pack.
\end{itemize}
