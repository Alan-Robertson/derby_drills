\subsection*{Weave $n$ Hit $k$}
\label{drill:pacelines/weave_hit}
% Pure NRG

Starting with a single paceline at a slow to moderate pace.

The rear-most skater should weave through the back $n$ skaters.
They should then approach the front of the line, hitting and driving $k$ skaters out by at least a lane along the way.
