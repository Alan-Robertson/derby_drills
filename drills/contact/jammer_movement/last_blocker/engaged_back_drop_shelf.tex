\subsection*{Spin Jab}
\label{drill:last_blocker/drop_shelf}

This drill is about throwing off a blocker's momentum to break out of an engagement.
This is best setup as if there is only a single blocker remaining, with their back facing towards the jammer with both skaters initially stationary.  

The jammer should start by setting up a shelf on the blocker - thigh underneath their thigh, foot placed horizontally under their centre of gravity, shoulder set up on their ribcage and hip under their hip creating a leverage point.  

The movement is then a slight lateral, without engaging toe-stops, into the blocker, lifting them slightly.
Once they're lifted, the second part of the movement is a fast disengagement, dropping the blocker and forcing their momenetum back in the opposite direction.  
While the blocker is falling the jammer then continues moving in the same direction as their shelf, hopefully fast enough that the blocker cannot correct their momentum in time.
