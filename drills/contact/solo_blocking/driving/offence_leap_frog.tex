\subsection*{Offence Leap Frog: Driving}
\label{drill:drives/leap_frog}

This is a driving variant of the leap frog.

Start with a skater in the centre of the track, rather than the line.

Perform a run-up into a drive on the skater, attempting to get them to the edge of the track.
Then reset in the centre, swapping such that the driven skater is now driving. 

Swap which skater is on the inside and outside.
