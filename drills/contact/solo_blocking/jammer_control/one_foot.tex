\subsection*{Drill: One Foot Drive}
\label{drill:one_on_one/one_foot_blocking}

This is more of a warm up drill, relating to one foot blocking.

One skater should attempt to drive the other down the track.
The other skater should adopt a partially sideway posture, and lift their back leg, blocking with just their front foot. 

Should the driving skater disengage, they should fall back onto their raised foot without losing position. 
Otherwise they should move their engaged foot in a C cut to control their movement down the track while being driven.
