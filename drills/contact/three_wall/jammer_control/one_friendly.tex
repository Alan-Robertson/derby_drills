\subsection*{Drill: Three Wall Cutting One Friendly}
\label{drill:three_wall/cutting_one_friendly}

This drill extends from a three wall pocket.
The idea here is to perform a bigger clearance of the jammer than is usually performed with the blocker intentionally taking themselves out.
The goal is then for the wall to reform in a controlled fashion without incurring a penalty.

Starting with the jammer pocketed on the line, the driving blocker should drive the jammer out, taking thesmelves with the jammer. 
One of the blockers (either the butt or the brace) should then perform a run-back, while the other maintains pack definition. 

The initiating blocker can now re-enter in front of the blocker performing a run-back (cutting one friendly blocker).
They can then join the other blocker at the back in a two-wall.  

Their next goal is to perform a two-wall catch, then reform with their third blocker as a three wall and repeat the drill. 



{\it Progressions:} Rather than starting from a pocket, begin with the jammer in the centre of the track.
Alternatively, or additionally, perform this drill with two packs, and avoid causing a destruction on the run back.
