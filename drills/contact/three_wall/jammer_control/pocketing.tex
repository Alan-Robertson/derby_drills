\subsection*{Drill: Three Wall Pocketing}
\label{drill:three_wall/pocket}

This drill extends from a three wall drive.
Starting in lane two or three, the jammer should be engaged with the wall. 
One of the blockers should then extend backwards slightly and attempt to either shelve or hook the jammer, then drive them towards the opposite line. 

As they approach the line the brace should double on the line, and the butt on the line should remain square. 

The driving blocker should drop and expose their back towards the in - presenting an illegal contact zone.
Ideally they would also extend one leg backwards, limiting the jammer's mobility in that direction. 

The wall should then hold this position while inching forward (to prevent a stopped block) for as long as possible, hitting the jammer out only if it is required.
