\subsection*{Drill: Shorty Says}
\label{drill:three_wall/shorty_says}

This drill was taken from a Shortstop bootcamp, hence the name.
It is listed as for three walls but can also be done solo or in a two wall.


The instructor provides a sequence of directions.
On the whistle the wall, should implement those directions as fast as possible. 

Examples of directions include:

\begin{itermize}
\item Move forward $x$ feet
\item Move backwards $x$ feet
\item Move to lane $x$ 
\item Rotate in your wall
\end{itemize}



{\it Progression: Offence}
While the wall is attempting to perform the required movements, an offensive skater should attempt to disrupt the wall.
