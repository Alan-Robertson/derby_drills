\subsection*{Drill: Sliding Doors}
\label{drill:three_wall/sliding_doors}


This drill requires a three-wall, an opposing jammer and two offence skaters.

One offence will set up to clear the brace, while the other takes the butts from the outside, opening a path for the jammer.

The rear offence should attempt to corral both of the butts, either with a skewer, or by covering the jammer after the first hit.    
The forward offence's goal is to remove the brace, thereby removing the wall's defense in depth.


The jammer can assist with this offence by juking in the opposite direction to their back offence.   
This disengages the wall's edges, as they track the jammer, and gives them momentum in the direction that the offence is about to drive them.
