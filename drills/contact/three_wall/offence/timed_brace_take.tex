\subsection*{Drill: Timed Brace Take}
\label{drill:three_wall/timed_brace_take}


This drill requires a jammer, a three wall and an offence, the jammer begins 10-15 feet behind the wall. 
The offence begins down-track from the wall. 

The idea is that the offence should remove the brace just before the jammer hits the wall, then use the offence as a screen while passing.

The jammer can perform a small juke to get the wall moving towards the opposite line to the offence - de-weighting the brace's edges for the offence, before targeting the opposite blocker to bust open the seam. 
The offence should drive the brace, ideally using the brace and themselves as a screen and leaving the jammer with a single blocker.
 

\end{minipage}
