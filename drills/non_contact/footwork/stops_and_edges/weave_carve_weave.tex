\subsection*{Drill: Weave, Carve, Weave}
\label{drill:footwork/weave_carve_weave}

Starting in the centre of the track with a runup.
The first motion is a weave towards either line, followed by a sharp carve back to the centre, followed by a corrective weave to continue the forwards direction of travel.  

This should resemble a possible jammer juke, but is mostly to stress the edges under speed.
