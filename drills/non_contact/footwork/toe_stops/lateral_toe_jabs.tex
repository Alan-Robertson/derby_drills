\subsection*{Drill: Lateral Toe Jabs}
\label{drill:footwork/lateral_toe_jabs}

% Grimes

Performed along a line as a referencence point.

\begin{itemize}
\item Toe jab over the line and back.
\item Grapevine step over the line, swapping sides. 
\item Toe jab back over the line
\item Grapevine step back to the original side
\item Repeat
\end{itemize}
