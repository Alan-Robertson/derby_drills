\subsection*{Drill: Spinning}

A progressive warm up drill.

Skating around the track start with moving between forwards skating, backwards skating, and inside and outside side surfs. 


For a stepped version:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Forward skating, to side surf 
    \item Side surf to backwards
    \item Backwards to other side surf
    \item Other side surf to fowards
\end{itemize}

For a stepped version:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Forward skating, carve into side surf 
    \item Side surf to backwards carve
    \item Backwards carve to other side surf
    \item Other side surf to fowards carve
\end{itemize}




Progress to constant motion between these movements in the form of a spin.
