\subsection*{Sharp Carves}
\label{drill:carves/sharp}

This drill focuses on using edges to sharpen the angle of a carve at speed.

\begin{itemize}
    \item Starting in the centre of the track, pick up a comfortable amount of speed 
    \item Perform a carve towards the outside, or inside line 
    \item Before reaching the line, perform a carve back towards the centre. 
    \item Before reaching the centre, straightening up to continue down the centre of the track. 
    \item Regain speed, and attempt the opposite line. 
\end{itemize}

The goal is for short, sharp carves that change direction, rather than longer carves. 

{\it Progressions:}
To progress this drill, increase speed, and shorten the turning distance.
