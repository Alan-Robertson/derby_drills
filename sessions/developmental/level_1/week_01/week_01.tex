\section*{Overview}

{\bf Training Preamble}
This is the first level one session. The goal is mostly a warm up and return to skating, with a secondary goal of spending some time working on the more difficult level 1 skills.



\section*{Preamble}

\subsection*{Off Skates Warm Up}

\begin{itemize}
\item Simple toe taps
\item Hip flexors 
\item Shoulder, arm, and neck stretches
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Safety Talk}

\subsection*{Gear Adjustment}

\subsection*{Standing Up \& Rolling}

\section*{Skating}

Introduction to eggshells.
Possibly open with an \hyperref[drill:eggshells/spot]{on the spot drill}for development.

\subsection*{Falling}
\begin{itemize}
\item Knee taps
\item Double knee falls
\item Turtle falls
\item Slide falls
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Plough Stops}

Introduction to plough stops.
The rolling back and forward \hyperref[drill:eggshells/spot]{drill} may help here.

\begin{itemize}
\item \hyperref[drill:plough_stops/rocking]{Rocking} into ploughs.
\item \hyperref[drill:plough_stops/cruising]{Cruising} into ploughs
\item For precoucious skaters - start making the \hyperref[drill:plough_stops/cruising]{ploughs active}.
\end{itemize}



\section*{Homework}
\begin{itemize}
\item Getting up without putting hands down
\item One Foot Balancing, 30 seconds, forwards and sideways
\end{itemize}
