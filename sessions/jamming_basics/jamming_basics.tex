\subsection*{Edgework Skills - 15 Minutes}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Carve surfing 
    \item Single foot inside and outside edges 
    \item Carves at speed 
    \item Lateral to carve
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Lateral Carve at the Line - 10 Minutes}

Starting engaged on the blocker - the jammer should lateral into carve to pass the blocker.
The carve should start before lanes 1 or 4, otherwise the jammer risks being simply pushed out.

Some ideas:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Pause into tempo for a sudden movement, j
    \item Pressing down on the blocker's arm near the elbow.
    \item Pushing forward near the elbow to turn the blocker.
\end{itemize}  

\subsection*{Pump Juke, Lateral Carve - 10 Minutes}
Mixing into the previous drill.
Threaten a pump juke, mixed up into the lateral carve movement.

At the end run a section with the blocker facing towards the jammer rather than facing away.

\subsection*{Juke on Approach}
Focusing on juke to hit, or hit to juke
Numbers depending, getting a rolling set of skaters.


\subsection*{Shelving and Driving}
Pin to juke on the three wall.
Using shelving or driving to pin, then juking.
