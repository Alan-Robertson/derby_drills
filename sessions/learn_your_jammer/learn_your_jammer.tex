This is a training sesion about jammer specific assists.
Also a more general team building session.

\section*{Overview}

\subsection*{Training Preamble - 15 minutes}
Warm-up, forwards and backwards with \hyperref[drill:sticky:eggshells]{Eggshells}, \hyperref[drill:one_foot:serpentine]{one foot drills} and  \hyperref[drill:carves_and_weaves]{edgework} - 5 Minutes

\subsection*{Transition Book Club - 15 minutes}

Everyone do a transition, everyone copy it.
Last 10 minutes, pick someone who did a transition you couldn't and pair up with them.

\subsection*{Footwork} - 20 minutes
\begin{itemize}
\item Side surf, alternate back and front foot
\item Lateral Leaps
\item Putting it together - lateral leaps, back foot, front foot 
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{One on One Blocking}
Warm up one-on-one blocking.

\subsection*{Learn your Jammer - Starts}

Jam starts, go until double lead, work in consistent packs. 
The goal is for the offense to learn at least two openings for the jammer.
Other blockers should simply attempt to block.

\subsection*{Learn your Jammer - In their Eyes}
At 5 seconds, pause the start, spectators guess their jammer's move's. 

\subsection*{Learn your Blockers - Scoring Points}
Jammer begins on a scoring pass, other jammer is half a track behind, blockers need to coordinate point scoring.
